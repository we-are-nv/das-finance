Meet the DAS team

Your one-stop-shop for mortgage and finance advice.

At DAS Finance, we opened our doors in Westcliff on Sea, Essex back in 2019, but we’re not new to the finance industry.

Our experienced, professional and approachable team has over 100 years of combined experience in delivering comprehensive financial solutions for all sizes of investments, from small businesses to large corporations.

We pride ourselves in keeping up to date with relevant legislation, as we refuse to become complacent in an ever-changing industry.

We pride ourselves in working closely with our clients, focusing firmly on your wants and needs. 

Get the answers to important questions when you need them.

Forget waiting around for the answers to pressing matters. At DAS Finance, we are at hand when you need us. We have a multitude of options for getting support, including email, phone, live-chat, online video calls or face-to-face visits; ensuring you're never left out of the loop.
